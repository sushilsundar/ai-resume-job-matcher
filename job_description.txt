About the job
We are seeking a highly analytical Data Analyst with strong experience in Agile environments and enterprise data ecosystems. The role focuses on translating business requirements into data-driven insights, supporting decision-making through structured analysis, and contributing to end-to-end analytical solutions.



Key Responsibilities

Identify, assess, and prepare data sources to support business and analytical requirements, including analysis of data availability, quality, and constraints
Conduct stakeholder workshops to capture analytical use cases, reporting needs, KPIs, and decision requirements
Profile, validate, and analyse data to identify trends, patterns, anomalies, and data quality issues
Define and document analytical requirements, KPIs, metrics, business rules, and data logic
Perform source-to-target mapping and support end-to-end data and reporting solution design
Develop dashboards, reports, and visualisations to generate insights and support business decisions
Manage analytics backlog including prioritisation of use cases, defining acceptance criteria, and aligning with business value
Support demos, reviews, and proof-of-concept activities, presenting findings and incorporating stakeholder feedback
Provide regular updates on progress, risks, dependencies, and data-related issues to project stakeholders
Contribute to solution design documentation and knowledge transfer activities


Key Requirements

Strong understanding of data analysis principles including data profiling, EDA, KPI definition, and data modelling concepts
Experience working with complex enterprise data environments, including data quality, integration, and technical constraints
Proven ability to translate business questions into analytical requirements and actionable insights
Hands-on experience with Agile delivery and tools such as Jira, Azure DevOps (ADO), or similar
Experience managing analytics backlogs, refining use cases, and prioritising work based on business value
Strong problem-solving skills with ability to identify root causes and data-driven recommendations
Understanding of data governance, security, and privacy including role-based access and sensitive data handling
Excellent communication skills with the ability to explain complex data concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
Ability to work independently in fast-paced environments and manage competing priorities


Nice to Have

Experience with dashboards and visualisation tools (Power BI, Tableau, etc.)
Exposure to data modelling, data warehousing, or analytics platforms
Experience contributing to solution design documentation
